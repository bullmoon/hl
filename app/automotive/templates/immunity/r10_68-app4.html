R10.06 / R10.07 - Annex 4

Description
This test is performed to ensure the immunity of the electronic system to radiated disturbances.

Calibration
  • The EUT and wiring harness shall be placed on a non-conductive support 50 mm above the ground plane.
  • The test shall be performed with the EUT in a shielded enclosure.
  • The antenna shall be placed 1 meter from the EUT.

Test
  • The test shall be performed over the frequency range 20 MHz to 2000 MHz.
  • The test level shall be 30 V/m for passenger cars and 20 V/m for commercial vehicles.
  • The test shall be performed with horizontal and vertical polarization.
