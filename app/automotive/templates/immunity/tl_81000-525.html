5.2.5 Mobile radio communications test

The requirements as per ISO 11452-9 apply.
Test conditions The applicable frequency range of the test method is 142 MHz to 6 GHz.
    Antennas:
  • HL 5439: 80 MHz - 3 GHz
  • HL 7710: 3 GHz - 6 GHz
    Amplifiers:
  • HL 5393: 80 MHz - 1000 MHz
  • HL 5394: 800 MHz - 3000 MHz
  • HL 5395: 2500 MHz - 6000 MHz

Pay attention to EMC32 doesn't support overlapping. You should create the limit files without overlapping, and templates according to these files.
