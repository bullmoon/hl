INTERNATIONAL STANDARD ISO 11452-9
Road vehicles — Component test methods for electrical disturbances from narrowband radiated electromagnetic energy
Part 9: Portable transmitters

Using software: R&S EMC32
Test conditions: The applicable frequency range of the test method is 142 MHz to 6 GHz.
Standard test conditions are given in ISO 11452‐1 for the following:
  • test temperature;
  • supply voltage;
  • dwell time;
  • test signal quality;
  • frequency steps;
  • modulation.
Test location: The test shall be performed in an absorber lined shielded enclosure (ALSE).
    Antennas:
  • (80 MHz - 3 GHz) HL 5439
  • (3 - 6 GHz) HL 7710

Set "Boonton 4220A" units of measurement to dB, not in W. Generator and Power Meter should use the same units of measurement.
Modulation.

Dwell time at each test frequency shall be 2 s or longer.
