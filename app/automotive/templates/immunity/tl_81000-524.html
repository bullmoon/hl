5.2.4 Stripline

Testing using the stripline method as per ISO 11452-5 is possible in exceptional cases. This may only be used following agreement with the purchaser.
    The useful frequency range of stripline is from 10 kHz to 400 MH.
    The minimum dwell time is 2 s. If the DUT reacts more slowly to interference, then the dwell time must be increased accordingly.
    The stripline method test must be performed with the maximum field strength as specified in figure 9. The FPSC must be performed as per table 13 and figure 10.
