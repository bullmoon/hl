The conducted emissions on power lines are measured successively on positive power supply and power return by connecting the measuring instrument on the measuring port of the related AN, with the measuring port of the AN in the other supply lines being terminated with a 50 Î© load.

    For voltage measurements the following apply:
  • For EUT remotely grounded (vehicle power return line longer than 200 mm), the voltage measurements shall be made on each lead (supply and return) relative to the reference ground plane (see Figure 9).
  • For EUT locally grounded (vehicle power return line 200 mm or shorter), voltage measurements on power supply leads shall be made relative to the reference ground plane (see Figure 10).
