6. Measurement of components and modules
6.5 Radiated emissions from components/modules - ALSE method

(This test will take a long time, because of QP. 200-1000 MHz test is executing for 6-7 hours)
    Antennas & ranges:
  • rod antenna test setup (150 kHz - 30 MHz)
  • biconical antenna test setup (30 - 200 MHz)
  • log periodic antenna test setup (200 - 1000 MHz)
  • horn antenna test setup (above 1000 MHz)
    It is extremely important to use Isolation Transformer (Otherwise, you can face with unexpected ambient noise)
    Up to 1 GHz:
  • Use Pre-Amplifier LNA 1187 for 30 MHz to 1 GHz (Internal Pre-Amplifier ESU40 must be disabled)
  • LNA 1187 has two inputs for different ranges
    Above 1 GHz:
  • Replace LNA HL 1187 (up to 1 GHz) to LNA HL 4932 (upper 1 GHz)
  • Rod Antenna must be connected with reference plane (Grounding connection (full width bond between counterpoise and reference ground plane) page 61, figure 15)
  • Use Pre-Amplifier LNA 1187 for 30 MHz to 1 GHz (Internal Pre-Amplifier ESU40 must be disabled)
  • Gain must be switched OFF
  • Use Pre-Amplifier LNA 1187 for 30 MHz to 1 GHz (Internal Pre-Amplifier ESU40 must be disabled)
  • ...
    Log periodic antenna
  • Use Pre-Amplifier LNA 1187 for 30 MHz to 1 GHz (Internal Pre-Amplifier ESU40 must be disabled)
  • ...
    Horn antenna (Double Ridged Horn Antennas only!)
  • Available antennas: HL 4114 (red), HL 4474 (blue), HL 4432 (grey)
